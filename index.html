© Alexander Tekle & Hack4Progress - Combining Technology & Social Good

Hack4Progress is all about technology and social good.

Here at Hack4Progress, we provide software and hardware support for other nonprofits, activists, and social workers who need assistance. We also provide Raspberry Pi computers and programming tutoring for youth to provide young scholars the tools to embark on a career in the tech-world.
